How to Play

Mark the state plates you spot. Get 5 in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — to win!

Game Modes

USA

All 50 states. Classic road-trip bingo.

East Coast

16 East Coast & Southeast states only. Faster games, regional challenge.

Kid Colors

9 colorful states for younger players.

Board Controls

New Board

Free Pick

Road Watch brings the road to you! Animated cars drive across the screen — when a car's plate matches a state on your board, it's automatically marked for you.

How It Works

Start

Tap the blue Start button in the Road Watch panel. Cars begin driving and plates appear above them.

Auto-Mark

When a car shows a state that's on your board, it's automatically marked. No tapping needed!

Stop

Tap Stop to end Road Watch mode. You can start it again anytime.

Tips

Music

Toggle the Music checkbox to control background road music independently from sound effects.

Board Synced

Road Watch marks cells on whatever board you currently have loaded (USA, East Coast, or Kid Colors).
