How to Play

Choose a help tab: Classic Board for real-world license plate spotting, or Road Watch for the computer-driven version.

Classic Board is the traditional road-trip game: watch real traffic, tap each plate you spot, and try to complete a winning pattern.

Goal

Complete a Pattern

Mark states as you see their plates. Score when you complete a straight line, diagonal, Four Corners, Postage Stamp, T shape, Perimeter, or Cover All. The hint line shows when you are one or two squares away from a pattern.

Playing the Board

Passengers Only

Do not play while driving. A passenger should operate the game and watch for plates.

Spot a Plate

Look for license plates on cars around you: passing traffic, parking lots, rest stops, or cars you pass on the road.

Tap to Mark

When you spot a state that appears on your board, tap that square. Tap it again if you marked it by mistake.

Free Play Center

The center Free Play square starts marked automatically and counts toward any pattern that crosses it.

Free Pick

Free Pick randomly marks one open license-plate square. It is available in USA and East Coast games.

Game Choices

USA

A full 50-state road-trip challenge using a 5-by-5 board.

East Coast

A faster regional game with East Coast and Southeast states.

Kid Colors

A simpler color-matching board for younger players. Road Watch is for license plate games only.

Controls & Sound

New Board

Creates a fresh shuffled board for the current game type. Your current marks and score reset for the new board.

Sound

Sound controls taps, Free Pick, wrong taps, already-marked states, and win effects. Music controls only the Road Watch background music.

Road Watch creates a virtual highway so you can play even when you are not in a car.

Goal

React to the Traffic

Start Road Watch, watch the animated cars, and claim the states that appear before they disappear. You are still trying to complete the same winning patterns as Classic Board.

How Road Watch Works

Start the Computer

Choose USA or East Coast, then tap Start in the Road Watch panel. Kid Colors does not use Road Watch.

Watch for the Active Car

Every few seconds, one car reveals a license plate and state name. The prompt above the board shows the plate, its countdown, and whether it is claimable or a decoy.

Claim the State

When a spotted state appears on your board, that square glows gold. Tap the glowing square or the active car before it disappears to mark it.

Misses & Already Marked States

If the computer shows a state you cannot claim, the status line warns you. Tapping a state you already marked plays a different sound and leaves the board unchanged.

Road Watch Rules

No Manual Hunting

While Road Watch is running, you cannot freely mark board squares. You can only claim the current glowing target, tap the active car, or use a Free Pick.

Difficulty Levels

Easy gives you more time and more board matches. Normal uses the standard pace. Hard moves faster, gives less claim time, and includes more decoys. A successful claim brings up the next plate quickly.

Three Free Picks

In Road Watch, Free Pick is limited to 3 uses. The button shows how many are left, then disables when you run out.

Stop Anytime

Tap Stop to clear the active target, restore normal board tapping, and leave Road Watch mode. Road Watch also pauses automatically whenever this page is hidden.

Sound & Music

Sound

Sound controls taps, Free Pick, wrong taps, already-marked states, and win effects.

Music

Music controls only the Road Watch background music. It plays while Road Watch is running and stops when Road Watch stops.
